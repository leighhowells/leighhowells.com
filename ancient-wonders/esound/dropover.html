EVP Dropover

Factory-built enclosures and canopies for machine skid or builders work plinth mounting.

These enclosures are built for large and small diverse applications. They can have EVS ventilation silencers, EVL louvres, access panels, doors, explosion panels and windows built in. Paint finish to suit is standard.

Mainly for machines, pumps, compressors, generators and a / c plant.


EVP Modular Enclosures

Complete enclosure or sectional modules can be factory built.

Some of the advantages and features are detailed below: -

  • A facility that allows for easy relocation and extension.
  • A start-up facility for initial trials
  • Fully weather-proofed for external applications.
  • Readily located outside on a simple hardstanding to minimise site building work.
  • Provides both acoustic and thermal insulation.
  • Units can have M&E services pre-fitted to minimise site works. This includes electrical controls, power, lighting, air conditioning and piped services.
  • The facility can be fully operational and commissioned within a short site working period.
  • Specialist plant can be mounted on the roof or in purpose-designed plantroom modules annexed to the facility.
