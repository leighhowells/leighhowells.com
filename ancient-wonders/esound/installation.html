Installation
This is often the most critical phase of any contract.

Valuable time can be lost with uncoordinated deliveries, problems of service interfacing and lack of site control.








 

Envirosound engineers maintain a close site control throughout this period and convene regular contract meetings for monitoring progress, making decisions and avoiding problems before they occur.

Our team of site erectors are experienced in Test Cell building and co-ordinate with other trades. This is important when services or routings are interdependent.

A commissioning service is normally provided to set up plant and equipment ready for system, engine or vehicle trials.
