EVP Panelwork

Designed to give an optimised noise reduction range from 25 – 45 dBA.

Simple, modular build allows for flat pack delivery and very quick site erect time. Ideal for areas where loading out to or access is a problem.

High quality finishes from pre coated steel to powdercoat / painted treatments.

The system utilises our proven 50/75/100 multi-layer panel construction, having an internal perforated facing of 0.7mm, layered acoustic wool infil of medium 45 to high 80 kg/m3 density, optional sound stop cores and an outer casing of 1 to 1.6mm. Panel modules are made to measure to a maximum width of 1,000mm and 2,400/3,000mm long. A special 3,300mm length is available.

The panels are assembled using channel / H & T joiner and roof capping sections. These are colour and trim matched or contrasted to suit.


EVP Pentapost

Using an integral RHS skeletal support framework, enclosures can be built as supporting / load bearing structures.

This is ideal for large span enclosures, where support to roof mounted plant is required or integral lifting beams / crane rails are required.

These units are normally site erected and then finished painted. Ideally combined with white Colorcoat perforated internals for good overall quality finish.
