Established in 1985, Envirosound is an Acoustic Engineering Company handling large multi-disciplined contracts.  We are a specialist supplier of Test Facilities, predominantly for the Automotive and Industrial sectors. Our systems include noise control, ventilation, air-conditioning systems, water cooling, exhaust systems, electrical control and wiring and miscellaneous back up services.

Being a specialist noise control contractor, we also design, build and install Acoustic Enclosures, Silencers, Louvres, Wall Linings, Doors and Windows for Industrial, Building Services, Studio and Office applications.

Our market is mainly the UK, but projects have been undertaken in the USA, France, Norway, Brazil and Sweden.

Survey & Analysis / Design

A full diagnostic survey and analysis is completed for both existing noise problems and new installation design schemes.  This can be extended as a consultancy or advisory service for factory, office or environment areas to record existing noise levels and identify problem or potential problem areas.
