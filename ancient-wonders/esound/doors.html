EVP 50 Wall / Ceiling Lining

This provides a good quality decorative finish combined with noise absorption.

The slim line panels are simply fixed with channel edge trims. Each application is purpose-designed, so if special joiners, cores etc are required these can be manufactured to suit.

The standard panels are available in white perforate colorcoat. Other finishes are standard industrial grade galvanised and powder coated / spray paint finishes. Trims normally match, but a contrast colour can be added for framing effect.

Where services are required to be covered, the lining system can be spaced from the wall or ceiling surface. An integral services trunking can be added with a wide surface fixed cover plate.

The system is ideal to add acoustic treatments to existing areas or to provide an integral part of a new facility.
