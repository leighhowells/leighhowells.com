Press Release February 1999

Exciting New Eventing Packages created by Keith Prowse International

Keith Prowse International, the worldwide entertainments and travel services company, have created exciting new packages for 1999 in the popular sport of three day eventing for the first time, at The Mitsubishi Motors Badminton Horse Trials / 6th - 9th May; The Chubb Insurance Windsor International Horse Trials / 27th - 30th May; The Burghley Pedigree Chum Horse Trials / 2nd - 5th September and The Blenheim Pet Plan International Horse Trials / 9th - 12th September.

Using their years of experience and contacts Keith Prowse have put together a comprehensive and approved package for each event including budget / tourist / first class accommodation choices, and through close cooperation with these popular British events, have included membership benefits to allow enthusiasts to be at the heart of the action and rub shoulders with owners and competitors. Air fares can also be arranged.

Visitors from the following countries can contact their location Keith Prowse office direct on the telephone - Australia, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Channel Islands, Czech Republic, France, Germany, Ireland, Netherlands, New Zealand, Northern Ireland, Norway, Scotland, Switzerland, United States and the United Kingdom or further information can be obtained by e-mail enquiry to jersey@keithprowse.com.

 

Press Release November 1998

The Chubb Insurance Windsor International Horse Trials.   Thursday 27th - Sunday 30th May 1999.  Spring Bank Holiday weekend in Windsor Great Park, owned by Her Majesty the Queen. 

The event attracts up-and-coming horses ridden by International and National riders competing at intermediate level.

Also hosting the National British Junior Championships for the leading 16-18 year olds.  

Thursday/FridayDressage
SaturdayCross-country, Roads and Tracks and Steeplechase
SundayShowjumping

There are ring entertainments, shops and refreshments throughout the event. 

Tickets may be pre-booked.

You may also become a Member of the horse-trials, which entitles entry for 2 people/1 car, and gives use of the Members' Tent. In addition you will also be given a chauffeur driven tour of the cross-country course  (Seats are provided in Members Only for showjumping - these are unreserved. There are no seats available for Dressage).

It is possible to combine a stay in Central London with this event or consider a 'traditional' hotel in the Windsor area.
