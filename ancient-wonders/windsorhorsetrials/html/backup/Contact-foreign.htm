Membership details

The package includes use of the member's tent. Plus! an unusual highlight is a chauffeur driven tour of the cross-country course in a four wheel drive vehicle. There are no seats for dressage but unreserved chairs in the 'members only' are available for the Show Jumping.

Hotels

Because of the nature of these events, we are unable to include precise accommodation details in this brochure. Some people may prefer to stay in larger towns close to the even and use public transfers on a daily basis. Others may prefer more rural and family orientated accommodation, where car hire will be necessary.

Our policy is simple. We offer you accommodation in three varying price ranges. You choose the price range to suit your needs and advise your preferences as to the 'type' of accommodation. We will do our utmost to satisfy those needs and will provide you with all the necessary details before you confirm your booking.
