The Windsor Horse Trials is divided into three classes. The first class is for the more experienced combinations. The second class is for less experienced combinations and the third class is for Juniors under 18 years.

Whilst Show Jumping and Cross Country scores can be calculated objectively, Dressage can only be scored subjectively.

Class division is used as each judge is only able to score 80 horses in total over the 2 days of the event.
