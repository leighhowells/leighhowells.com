Each glasshouse is designed individually,
in step with its intended use, whilst complementing its surroundings.

From glasshouses that attach to the
wall - called lean-to's - to those that
are free standing. Glasshouses buried
partly in the ground - called pit houses -
to ones with limited glass areas for Ferns.
From rebuilding glasshouses on existing
bases and ensuring that the character
of the garden is not changed, to
developing greenfield sites... Alitex has
built them all.


 

 

 

 

 

What is more, our glasshouses can be
built anywhere in the world - in the hot
climates of California or Bodrum, Turkey,
to the Snow Falls in new Hampshire or
Geneva, Switzerland... whatever the
extremes of weather, Alitex glasshouses
retain their high quality and soundness.
