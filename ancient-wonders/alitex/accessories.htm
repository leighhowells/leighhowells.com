Alitex has worked with gardeners and
skilled horticulturalists throughout the
UK to provide a range of accessories to
assist you in achieving the full growing
potential from your glasshouse.

Made to the same high standards as
the glasshouses, you can choose from
benching with heavy cast aluminium
legs with rounded corners, to hanging
shelves, propagators, lights and potting
shoes plus much more.

Propogators in the Alitex livery are
available with a unique canopy and
heated mat, so you can start the season
early. Whilst cold frames and shading
help extend the growing season.

Any accessories in the range can be incorporated at the time the glasshouse
is built, or added later. Overseas customers
are advised to telephone or email for
details of our minimum order value when
ordering smaller items.
