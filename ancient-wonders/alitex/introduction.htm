In the most beautiful gardens across
the world, you will find Alitex
glasshouses, built in traditional style.

At Alitex, we have been building
traditional glasshouses for more
than 50 years... meeting our
customer's aspirations for structures
that are stylish yet highly functional..
just as they were in a bygone age.
In fact, we recently assembled a
group of specialists - Garden
Designers, Architects, Historians
and Head Gardeners - and charged
them with evaluating our products
to ensure that our modern aluminium glasshouses are consistent with
traditional structures and in step
with our customer's tastes.

It is this attention to detail that
has made Alitex the choice of country
estates, private houses, universities, agricultural colleges, public gardens, embassies, hotels and leisure parks, including Royal gardens and the Royal Horticultural Society's own gardens in
Wisely in Surrey, England.
