Careful selection of materials ensures
a long and trouble-free life for your
glasshouse. Aluminium is the main
structural component held together
with stainless steel fastenings.
Glazing sections are light but strong
with reinforcement on the gutters,
bottom rail and ridge.

For a lasting finish, all aluminium
sections are coloured using the latest
powder coating technology. Our ethos
is to build once and build well.

To retain the traditional look, doors
are wooden but modified with an
external shell of fibreglass to prevent
any swelling or shrinkage. Our doors
have also been approved on UK sites
where there are Listed buildings.
