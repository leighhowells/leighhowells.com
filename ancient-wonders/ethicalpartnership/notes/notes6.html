6) Environmental Initiatives
6.1 Have a public environmental statement, or have provided EIRIS with a stand-alone environmental report, or have had a stand-alone environmental report accepted in the ACCA Environmental Reporting Awards.

6.2 Support the ICC Business Charter for sustainable development at parent company level, or include a part that is a member of the CBI Environment Business Forum.

6.3 Include a part that has received B57750, EMAS or 1S014001 certification.

6.4 Include a part that has been accredited under the energy efficiency accreditation scheme.

6.5 Have a part that has won or been commended in environmental award schemes in any one of the last three years for which they have been named.

6.6 Have made clear they develop or make direct use of wind, wave, solar or geothermal energy sources in the UK.

6.7 Have made clear that they have sponsored conservation projects in the UK in the last 3 years.
