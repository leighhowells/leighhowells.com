10) Ethical Issues

Alcohol - derive any turnover from the production of alcoholic drinks.

Biotechnology - carry out the genetic engineering or cloning of animals for medical purposes or xenotransplantation.

Gambling - derive more than 3% of their reported annual turnover from gambling.

Genetic engineering - manufacture food products containing ingredients derived from soya or maize, which have poor GMO avoidance and labelling policy, or have refused to reply to EIRIS.

Military - have been involved in the sale or production of weapons systems.

Pornography - publish, print or wholesale magazines that CPC says contain pornographic material or newspapers that have a photograph of a topless woman.

Tobacco - derive more than 10% of their reported annual turnover from the production or sale of tobacco or tobacco products.

Water pollution - have exceeded their discharge consents in the last year covered more than 35 times.

Greenhouse Gases - derive more than 33% of their reported annual turnover from the fossil fuel industries.

Health and Safety - have been convicted during the period covered and fined more than £4000 on at least one occasions following a prosecution by the Health and Safety Executive.

Human Rights Abuses - include a subsidiary or associated company registered in at least five of the countries, which Freedom House has given a rating of 6.5 or worse.

Military MOD Contracts - had contracts with the Ministry of Defence worth more than £25m in any one of the last 3 years.

Nuclear Power - have derived any turnover from the uranium fuel cycle, handle radioactive waste, or supply equipment or services other than safety or security equipment for nuclear plant or facilities.

Ozone Depletion - manufacture or supply products containing ozone-depleting chemicals (ODCs).

Pesticides - have received approval from MAFF or HSE to market pesticide products in the UK, which include active ingredients that are banned or restricted in 5 or more countries.

Pollution Convictions - have been convicted during the period covered following a prosecution by Her Majesty's Inspectorate of Pollution in England and Wales or Her Majesty's Industrial Pollution Inspectorate in Scotland.

Pornographic Films - provide adult entertainment television services.

Roads- have major interests in road vehicle use.

Tropical Hardwood - import, process or merchant timber, unless tropical -hardwood-free or from sustainable sources.
