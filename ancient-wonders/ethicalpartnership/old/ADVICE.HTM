The ethical partnership consists of a nationwide group of 'like minded' independent financial
advisers who specialise in financial planning. We will assess your ethical and environmental stance and offer financial solutions to meet both your financial planning and ethical objectives.



 

 

Our aim is to add value to client's lives, by improving the quality of benefits and protection, through offering constant and consistent service in accordance with each client's values.

Partners in the ethical partnership are founding members of the Ethical Investment Association whose aim is to adhere to a common code of practice, meet regularly with the ethical fund managers, ensure continuing professional development and promote ethical investment to the general public. Our partners are also members of ACIFA (Association of Christian Independent Financial Advisers).

We are also members of the UK Social Investment Forum and their website www.uksif.org is well worth visiting.

The ethical partnership is also a member of IFA Network Ltd which is regulated by the Personal Investment Authority.
