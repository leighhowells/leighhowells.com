The ethical partnership consists of a nationwide co-operative of 'like minded' independent financial advisers who specialise in financial planning linked to ethical and socially responsible investment. We will provide solutions to meet both your financial planning and ethical / socially responsible objectives.

Our aim is to add value to client's lives by improving the quality of benefits and protection, through offering a constant and consistent service in accordance with each client's values.

The ethical partnership offers over sixty years of financial services experience, including over 20 years in ethical investment.

Founding Partners in the ethical partnership are founding members of the EIA (Ethical Investment Association) whose website can be found at www.ethicalinvestment.org.uk. Their aim is to adhere to a common code of practice, meet regularly with the ethical fund managers, ensure continuing professional development and promote ethical investment to IFA's and the general public.

Partners are members of ACIFA (Association of Christian Independent Financial advisers), and of UKSIF (UK Social Investment Forum) whose website can be found at www.uksif.org.

The ethical partnership is also a member of AIFA (The Association of Independent Financial advisers) and IFA Network Ltd which is regulated by the Personal Investment Authority.

For further information on a variety of ethical and socially responsible matters please visit the ethical junction.
