Maendeleo Ya Jamii

 

Institutional Member

 

Maendeleo Ya Jamii is a non-governmental organization working to identify the needs of women and children and reduce poverty in the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC).  Specifically, the organization focuses on promoting social, economic and cultural rights of women and children.

 

Maendeleo Ya Jamii was established by two students who were moved by the precarious situation of women and their families affected by war and poverty in Eastern DRC. The founders conducted a study on the socio-economic situation faced by Congolese women in war zones and based on these results they organized a fundraising and awareness raising campaign in Italy.

 

To find out more about the organization, please visit their Facebook page and write to membership@awid.org

Manuella Donato

 

Individual Member

 

Manuella is from Pernambuco, Brazil. She holds Masters’ degrees in International Relations and Political Science.  Currently, Manuella is a lecturer at the Faculdade Asces where she is part of a Gender Studies group.

 

Since 2007, Manuella has been engaged in work on women’s rights, especially as it relates to sexual and reproductive health and rights (SRHRs). Her past experience includes working for Gestos, a Brazilian non-governmental organization focusing on HIV and AIDS and gender issues, and being a member of the Global Youth Coalition on HIV/AIDS. 

 

At the present time, Manuella is also a member of the Coletivo Mangueiras, a group of young people struggling for SRHRs, and the Fórum de Mulheres de Pernambuco, a feminist network for women’s rights. Manuella is looking forward to connecting with other AWID Members.

 

To contact Manuella, please email membership@awid.org
