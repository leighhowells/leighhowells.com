Post 2015 Development Agenda

 

This special focus section on the Post 2015 Development Agenda aims to shed light on the process underway to shape a new development agenda to succeed the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) when they expire in 2015. The section includes critical feminist analysis on the process and key resources including news, publications, statements, campaigns and events.

Mary Crane Derr Dies

12th June 2014

 

Sad times after 30 years service

We remember activist Mary Crane Derr after her hard work for our cause from 30 years dedicated work.  Mary left from London for Auckland on the ship City of Auckland in 1972. She was 16 years old, but her age is given as 20. Early that year, it is recorded that she was to travel on another ship, being sponsored by her brother Richard living in Thames, New Zealand.
