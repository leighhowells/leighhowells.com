Understanding Dyslexia: Identification, Curriculum and Teaching

Module: EDUM053
Venue: The University of Northampton
Module Leader: Barry Groom
30 Level M CATS Points

This module provides opportunities for students to build on key issues underpinning the effective provision for pupils and young people with Behavioural, Emotional and Social Difficulties (BESD), to explore aspects of current policies and the contexts and orientations which inform them.

The focus of this module is to develop students' knowledge and understanding of leadership and management approaches to BESD that incorporates a Children Services perspective. The module aligns itself closely with emergent thinking at national level regarding the implications of the Behaviour and Attendance initiatives (Key Stage 1-4) on the wider workforce, and, in particular, the impact of these on leadership in this field.
