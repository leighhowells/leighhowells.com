It's easy to take things for granted. But when you start looking closer at natural structures using designer eyes, their complexity, intricacy and beauty can suddenly become overwhelming.

Imagine being given a brief to create a portable shelter for a soft bodied organism to protect it from life in the ocean, to include protection from predators. I could never have begun to imagine the designs that evolution/divinity (delete as you see fit) has come up with.

Just look at them ! They are staggeringly complex, beautiful and hugely varied. Breathtaking.
